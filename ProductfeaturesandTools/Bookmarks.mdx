## **📌 What Is the Bookmarks Feature?**

The **Bookmarks** feature helps you quickly **save coding practice questions or assignment problems** that you want to revisit later. Whether it’s a tricky DSA problem or a coding question you want to review before your next cycle or interview—just hit the bookmark icon, and it’ll be saved for easy access.

❗Note: You can only bookmark **coding and assignment questions**. Sessions, cheat sheets, and other materials cannot be bookmarked.

---

## **🎯 Why Use Bookmarks?**

We know you come across questions worth reviewing again later. The **Bookmarks tab** gives you a clean, organized space to collect them—so you don’t waste time searching through everything all over again.

Use it for:

- Revision before assessments
- End-of-cycle reviews
- Interview prep

---

## **🛠️ How to Use It**

Here’s how to start bookmarking:

1. **While solving a coding or assignment question**, look for the **bookmark icon** (usually at the top of the page).
2. **Click the icon** to save the question—no extra steps needed.
3. Go to the **Bookmarks tab** in your Learning Portal's left-hand menu. Or go directly to[<u>learning.ccbp.in/bookmarks</u>](https://learning.ccbp.in/bookmarks) .
4. You’ll see a list of all your bookmarked questions. Just click on any to revisit it instantly.

💡 Hover over “What is My Bookmarks?” on the page to learn more about what can and can’t be bookmarked.

## **🌟 Why You’ll Love It**

- Easily track **challenging or high-value questions**
- No more digging through past modules to find that one tough problem

## **🧠 Best Practices**

- Bookmark questions you genuinely want to **master or revisit**
- Remove items you’ve already covered to keep your list clean
- Use your bookmark list regularly during **weekly reviews** or **interview prep sprints**
